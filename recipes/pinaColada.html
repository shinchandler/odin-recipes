Pi単a Colada

Description

The Pi単a Colada is a classic tropical cocktail known for its creamy, fruity flavor that instantly transports you to a beach paradise.
Blending pineapple juice, coconut cream, and rum, this drink is smooth, refreshing, and indulgently sweet, often served over ice and garnished with a wedge of pineapple and a cherry.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz (60 ml) white rum
  • 1 oz (30 ml) coconut cream
  • 3 oz (90 ml) pineapple juice
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • Pineapple wedge (for garnish)
  • Maraschino cherry (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Add rum, coconut cream, pineapple juice, and crushed ice into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a chilled glass, preferably a hurricane or highball glass.
  4. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a maraschino cherry.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the tropical vibes!
